Does Envoy on Windows support FIPS?Â¶

Envoy uses BoringSSL which is a slimmed down TLS implementation. At the time of writing, BoringSSL does not support a FIPS mode on Windows. As a result, Envoy does not offer support for FIPS on Windows.

If FIPS is a requirement for you, please reach out to the contributors.
